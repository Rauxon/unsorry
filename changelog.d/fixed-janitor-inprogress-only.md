Made the stale-run janitor catch zombies faster and stop harming the backlog: it now cancels `in_progress` Gate A runs after 60 min (down from 90 — well above the 45-min cold-build timeout, so real runs are safe) and **no longer cancels queued runs by default**. Under a backlog, runs queue legitimately for a long time waiting for a free runner; cancelling them only forced a re-run (churn) and freed no runner, while truly-superseded queued runs are already auto-cancelled by Gate A's cancel-in-progress concurrency. The janitor now only frees runners stuck on dead/preempted (`in_progress`) runs, which is what actually unclogs the pool.
