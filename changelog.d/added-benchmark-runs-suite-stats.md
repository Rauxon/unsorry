Added per-run benchmark telemetry for the guild benchmark surfaces. New `docs/metrics/benchmark-runs.json` joins every `proof-runs/` record to its registered suite (contributor, model, date, solve time, pass/fail, kernel verification), and `registered-targets.json` suites now carry `top` + `run_snippet` (run the WHOLE suite with one goal id, e.g. `putnam-v1-suite`) plus a `stats` summary (run counts, success rate, best/worst/median solve time). Retired suites are excluded from both. Feeds the suite card summary and the new suite detail page. (#5643.)
