**Finalization-recovery janitor (ADR-105 / SPEC-105-A).** Recovers stranded *mergeable* prove PRs the swarm's finalization step misses — the real merge bottleneck (Gate A/B compute has slack; completed work strands *after* the checks). Two conservative, bounded passes via `tools.repo.finalization_recovery`: (1) **arm** auto-merge on same-repo prove PRs left un-enrolled (`fork-automerge-enabler` only covers cross-repo), and (2) **re-run** `gate-a` runs that failed *only* because a sub-job was cancelled by the concurrency `cancel-in-progress` (a genuine proof failure or an `admission` policy block is left alone). No gate is bypassed — arming respects required checks, rerun only re-runs Gate A. `cancel-in-progress: false` so the merge firehose coalesces the sweep instead of cancelling it. Refs #5678.
