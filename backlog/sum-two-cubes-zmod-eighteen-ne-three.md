# sum-two-cubes-zmod-eighteen-ne-three

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 3 + b ^ 3 is never congruent to 3 modulo 18.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two cubes).
- **Reference:** 3 is not a sum of two cubes modulo 18; provable by a finite `ZMod 18` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 3
