# telescoping-quartic-sum-coeff-twentyeight

A telescoping power-sum closed form (coefficient 28, exponent 4): the finite sum over k in range n equals 28·n^4.

- **Source:** self-seeded telescoping finite-sum identity family.
- **Reference:** follows from (k+1)^4 − k^4 telescoping; proved by induction on n.
- **Difficulty:** 4
