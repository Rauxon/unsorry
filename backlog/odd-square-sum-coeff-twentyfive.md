# odd-square-sum-coeff-twentyfive

A scaled odd-square-sum closed form (coefficient 25): three times the sum over k in range n of 25·(2k+1)² equals 25·n·(2n−1)·(2n+1).

- **Source:** self-seeded odd-square-sum identity family.
- **Reference:** sum of the first n odd squares; proved by induction on n.
- **Difficulty:** 4
