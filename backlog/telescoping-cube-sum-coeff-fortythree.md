# telescoping-cube-sum-coeff-fortythree

A telescoping power-sum closed form (coefficient 43, exponent 3): the finite sum over k in range n equals 43·n^3.

- **Source:** self-seeded telescoping finite-sum identity family.
- **Reference:** follows from (k+1)^3 − k^3 telescoping; proved by induction on n.
- **Difficulty:** 4
