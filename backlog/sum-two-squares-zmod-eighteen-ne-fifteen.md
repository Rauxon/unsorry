# sum-two-squares-zmod-eighteen-ne-fifteen

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 2 + b ^ 2 is never congruent to 15 modulo 18.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two squares).
- **Reference:** 15 is not a sum of two squares modulo 18; provable by a finite `ZMod 18` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 3
