# sum-two-cubes-zmod-twentyone-ne-eighteen

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 3 + b ^ 3 is never congruent to 18 modulo 21.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two cubes).
- **Reference:** 18 is not a sum of two cubes modulo 21; provable by a finite `ZMod 21` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 3
