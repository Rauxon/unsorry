# telescoping-square-sum-coeff-sixtyfour

A telescoping power-sum closed form (coefficient 64, exponent 2): the finite sum over k in range n equals 64·n^2.

- **Source:** self-seeded telescoping finite-sum identity family.
- **Reference:** follows from (k+1)^2 − k^2 telescoping; proved by induction on n.
- **Difficulty:** 1
