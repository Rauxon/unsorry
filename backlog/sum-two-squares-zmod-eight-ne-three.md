# sum-two-squares-zmod-eight-ne-three

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 2 + b ^ 2 is never congruent to 3 modulo 8.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two squares).
- **Reference:** 3 is not a sum of two squares modulo 8; provable by a finite `ZMod 8` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 3
