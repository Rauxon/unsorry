# sum-two-squares-zmod-sixteen-ne-twelve

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 2 + b ^ 2 is never congruent to 12 modulo 16.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two squares).
- **Reference:** 12 is not a sum of two squares modulo 16; provable by a finite `ZMod 16` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 3
