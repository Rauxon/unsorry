# gbinom-falling-five-fact-dvd

The falling factorial n*(n-1)*...*(n-4) is always divisible by 5! = 120, for every integer n. This is the classical C(n,5) = n!/(n-5)!/5! integrality identity.

- **Source:** self-seeded polynomial-divisibility identity family.
