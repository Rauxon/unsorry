# telescoping-quintic-sum-coeff-sixtytwo

A telescoping power-sum closed form (coefficient 62, exponent 5): the finite sum over k in range n equals 62·n^5.

- **Source:** self-seeded telescoping finite-sum identity family.
- **Reference:** follows from (k+1)^5 − k^5 telescoping; proved by induction on n.
