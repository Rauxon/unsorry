# sum-two-squares-zmod-twentyfour-ne-twentythree

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 2 + b ^ 2 is never congruent to 23 modulo 24.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two squares).
- **Reference:** 23 is not a sum of two squares modulo 24; provable by a finite `ZMod 24` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 3
