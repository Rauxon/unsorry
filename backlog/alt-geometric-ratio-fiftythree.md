# alt-geometric-ratio-fiftythree

An alternating geometric series closed form (ratio -53): (53+1) times the sum over k in range n of (-53)^k equals 1 - (-53)^n.

- **Source:** self-seeded alternating-geometric identity family.
- **Reference:** geometric telescoping with ratio -r; proved by induction on n.
- **Difficulty:** 5
