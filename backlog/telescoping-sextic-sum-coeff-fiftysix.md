# telescoping-sextic-sum-coeff-fiftysix

A telescoping power-sum closed form (coefficient 56, exponent 6): the finite sum over k in range n equals 56·n^6.

- **Source:** self-seeded telescoping finite-sum identity family.
- **Reference:** follows from (k+1)^6 − k^6 telescoping; proved by induction on n.
