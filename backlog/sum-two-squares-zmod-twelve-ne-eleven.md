# sum-two-squares-zmod-twelve-ne-eleven

For all integers a, b, the sum a² + b² is never congruent to 11 modulo 12.

- **Source:** self-seeded quadratic-residue identity family.
- **Reference:** 11 is not a sum of two squares modulo 12; provable by a finite `ZMod 12` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 2
