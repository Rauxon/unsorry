# sum-two-cubes-zmod-seven-ne-four

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 3 + b ^ 3 is never congruent to 4 modulo 7.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two cubes).
- **Reference:** 4 is not a sum of two cubes modulo 7; provable by a finite `ZMod 7` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 1
