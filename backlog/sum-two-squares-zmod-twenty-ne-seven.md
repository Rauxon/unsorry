# sum-two-squares-zmod-twenty-ne-seven

For all integers a, b, the sum a ^ 2 + b ^ 2 is never congruent to 7 modulo 20.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (two squares).
- **Reference:** 7 is not a sum of two squares modulo 20; provable by a finite `ZMod 20` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 3
