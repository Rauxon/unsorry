# sum-three-squares-zmod-sixteen-ne-seven

For all integers a, b, c, the sum a ^ 2 + b ^ 2 + c ^ 2 is never congruent to 7 modulo 16.

- **Source:** self-seeded power-residue identity family (three squares).
- **Reference:** 7 is not a sum of three squares modulo 16; provable by a finite `ZMod 16` case check.
- **Difficulty:** 2
