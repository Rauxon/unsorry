# alt-geometric-ratio-thirtythree

An alternating geometric series closed form (ratio -33): (33+1) times the sum over k in range n of (-33)^k equals 1 - (-33)^n.

- **Source:** self-seeded alternating-geometric identity family.
- **Reference:** geometric telescoping with ratio -r; proved by induction on n.
- **Difficulty:** 5
